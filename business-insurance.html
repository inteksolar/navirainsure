Common Questions

Business Insurance FAQs

Businesses may face risks involving property, stock, employees, goods in transit and liabilities. Suitable insurance can provide protection against covered losses, subject to policy terms and conditions.

Depending on the selected policy, eligible property such as buildings, machinery, stock and contents may be insured against specified covered perils. Exact coverage depends on policy wording and conditions.

Marine cargo insurance can include eligible goods transported through various modes depending on the selected policy, transit route and coverage terms.

Eligible organisations can explore group health insurance options for employees or members. Coverage, eligibility and benefits depend on the selected insurer and policy terms.

The suitable insurance mix depends on factors such as the type of business, property, machinery, stock, employees, goods movement and liability exposure. Requirements should be assessed individually.
