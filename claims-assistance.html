Documents

Common Documents for Insurance Claims

Actual document requirements vary by insurer, policy type and claim circumstances. The following are common examples.

Motor Claim

  • Policy details
  • Driving licence
  • Registration certificate
  • Damage photographs, if required
  • Claim form and applicable records

Health Claim

  • Policy or health card details
  • Hospital records
  • Bills and payment receipts
  • Prescriptions and reports
  • Claim form and required documents

Life Claim

  • Policy details
  • Nominee identification
  • Applicable certificates or records
  • Bank details as applicable
  • Claim forms and supporting documents

This is a general indicative list. Additional documents may be required depending on the insurer, policy terms, claim type and circumstances.
