Key Coverage Areas

What Can Health Insurance Help Cover?

Coverage differs between policies. Depending on the selected plan and its terms, health insurance may provide coverage for several medical expenses.

Hospitalisation Expenses

Eligible expenses related to covered hospitalisation and medical treatment.

Pre & Post Hospitalisation

Eligible medical expenses before and after hospitalisation for specified periods.

Ambulance Expenses

Eligible ambulance charges may be covered as specified under the policy terms.

Day Care Procedures

Specified procedures that do not require traditional 24-hour hospitalisation.
