Common Questions

Life Insurance FAQs

Life insurance can help provide financial protection to dependants in the event of the insured person's death, subject to the selected policy terms and conditions. Certain life insurance products may also include savings or investment-related features.

Term insurance is primarily a protection-focused life insurance product that provides life cover for a specified policy term, subject to the terms and conditions of the selected policy.

The appropriate amount depends on several factors, including income, dependants, outstanding liabilities, existing assets, future financial goals and personal circumstances.

ULIPs include a life insurance component and a market-linked investment component. Investment value can be affected by market performance, charges and other product-specific terms.

Different insurance and long-term planning solutions may be available for goals such as children's future planning and retirement preparation. Suitability depends on your financial circumstances, goals and product terms.
