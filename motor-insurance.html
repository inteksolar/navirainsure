COVER OPTIONS

Understand Your Motor Insurance Options

Third-Party Cover

Covers applicable legal liability toward third parties according to policy terms and applicable law.

Comprehensive Cover

Combines applicable third-party liability cover with own-damage protection subject to policy terms.

Own Damage Cover

Protection for eligible damage to the insured vehicle from covered events according to policy conditions.
